Welcome to the BBC Quiz Portal

The Back to the Bible Challenge (BBC) is a digital Bible quiz competition for higher institution students across Kwara State, organized by Oasis of Wisdom Bible Church.

📝 Quiz Structure:

  • 📚 10 Multiple Choice Bible Questions
  • ⏱ 15 seconds per question (auto-switch or manually click “Next”)
  • 📦 Quiz auto-submits at the end or upon rule violations

🔒 Security & Integrity:

  • 🎥 Webcam required — you must remain visible during the quiz
  • 🎙️ Microphone detection — avoid background noise
  • 📵 Tab switch detection — switching ends your test

⚠️ Rules:

  • Do not switch browser tabs or open new apps
  • Ensure your camera and microphone are turned on
  • Do not get external help or background assistance
  • Answer honestly and independently
