Back to the Bible Challenge (BBC)

The BBC is a Bible-based quiz initiative organized by Oasis of Wisdom Bible Church, designed to challenge and reward Higher Institution students in Kwara State who are passionate about God’s Word. Participants study selected portions of Scripture and compete digitally via this platform.

Instructions & Guidelines:

  • Fill in your Full Name and Exam Number before starting.
  • Each quiz has 10 multiple-choice questions (A–D).
  • You have 15 seconds per question.
  • Once you select an answer, you can click Next or wait for it to auto-advance.
  • Do not switch tabs – it will auto-submit the quiz as malpractice.
  • Camera and microphone access will be requested for proctoring.
  • Attempt only when ready. You can only submit once.
