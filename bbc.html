Back to the Bible Challenge (BBC)

About the Program

The Back to the Bible Challenge (BBC) is an annual Bible quiz organized by Oasis of Wisdom Bible Church. It is designed for higher institution students across Kwara State and beyond to deepen their knowledge of the Scriptures and strengthen their walk with God.

Participants are given specific books of the Bible to study, and the final quiz tests their comprehension, speed, and accuracy. This year, we're taking it digital to allow broader participation and ensure a fair, monitored quiz environment online.
