Mighty Men of David Convention 2025: A Weekend of Impact, Innovation, and Inspiration

This past weekend, the Mighty Men of David (MMD) of Oasis of Wisdom Bible Church held their highly anticipated annual convention, and it was nothing short of powerful, impactful, and fun-filled. From Friday, June 27th to Sunday, June 29th, 2025, the church was alive with energy, learning, and the power of God.



Friday: Web Design Workshop

The convention kicked off on Friday with an exciting Web Design Workshop, where participants explored the basics of building and managing a modern website. In a hands-on session led by the MMD team, the men collaborated to create something truly special ‚Äî a new website for the church. We're thrilled to announce that the official Oasis of Wisdom Bible Church website is now live and functional!





Saturday: Games, Fellowship, and Refreshments

Saturday was all about fun, fellowship, and bonding. The church premises turned into a lively ground with gaming sessions on the PlayStation 4, intense rounds of table tennis, and a variety of snacks and music to keep the vibes up. It was a day to relax, connect, and enjoy the brotherhood of faith.
